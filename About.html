About Me

My name is Akshay Kollur, and I am a passionate programmer and first-year undergraduate student at The Ohio State University majoring in Computer Science. I am motivated to learn and grow both personally and professionally.

I love to play basketball, pickleball, and piano. When I am not doing all this, you can find me working on my projects. My projects consist of websites, mobile applications, and AI models. I was also a member of a Vex Robotics club throughout high school both aiding my team to advance to higher levels of competition and helping lead the club as a Treasurer.

I have four years of programming experience. I am fluent in Java, Python, C++, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have worked with the cloud specifically AWS, having earned my AWS Cloud Practitioner certification earlier this year.
